ICE London 2024 Recap

Olivia Brown

.

Feb 26, 2024

.

3 mins read

.
Post Views: 45
.
Listen

The ice melted in London, but Digitain ignited a firestorm of excitement at ICE London 2024!  

From February 6th to 8th, our team showcased a dynamic array of fantastic products and solutions, attracting industry leaders and forging partnerships that will propel the future of iGaming. The atmosphere was electrifying, the innovations were groundbreaking, and the networking opportunities were unparalleled at ICE London 2024.  

As Digitain once again took center stage, the event witnessed a convergence of industry leaders, innovative technologies, and legendary moments. 

Under the theme “YOUR SUCCESS,” we unveiled revolutionary offerings that are set to elevate your operations and push the boundaries of possibility.  

Here are some highlights from our electrifying presence at Stand S4-150: 

Game-Changing Solutions 

  • Digitain Sportsbook: An award-winning excellence that redefines the rules of the game, packed with extensive market coverage, tailored solutions, and groundbreaking features. 
  • Centrivo Platform: Our feature-rich, all-in-one iGaming platform offers seamless operations and maximizing efficiency. A flexible choice from Centrivo Universal or Centrivo Dynamics will definitely match your unique needs. 
  • Paydrom Payment Gateway: Securing and making your payment ecosystem much easier, our comprehensive solution integrates over 60 global providers and 600+ methods for seamless, streamlined transactions and enhanced player convenience. 
  • Virtual Sports: Your ultimate adventure with over 10 captivating games, 20,000+ daily events, and 150+ markets spanning football, horse racing, and more. Beyond realistic 3D world accessible on Web, Mobile, and Retail platforms. 

We’ve also showcased our Digitain App: Stay connected and informed with our convenient mobile app, offering seamless communication, product updates, news, and events – all at your fingertips. The Digitain App changes the way industry professionals engage with iGaming technology, providing easy access to company information and the latest developments in the industry. Whether you’re on the go or in the office, stay ahead of the curve with real-time updates and notifications. Download it on the App Store or Google Play today and experience the future of iGaming at your convenience. 

Beyond the Products 

Digitain made ICE London 2024 even more grandiose with exciting product announcements, special demonstrations, and exclusive offers throughout the event. 

 Attendees had the chance to: 

  • Experience firsthand the power of Digitain’s innovative solutions. 
  • Network with industry experts and discuss the future of iGaming. 
  • Discover how Digitain can help achieve their business goals. 

Legendary Moments 

Attendees witnessed history as Galácticos Legends Luís Figo and Michel Salgado united at Stand S4-150. Our stand was so insanely crowded when they attended! Additionally, we’ve organized an interesting Q&A session, where legends were answering journalists’ questions. Engaging with attendees, surprise raffles, and exclusive signed memorabilia added to the excitement. The raffle to win the signed jersey had 650+ participants! 

Luxury Watch Giveaway 

Digitain offered attendees the exclusive opportunity to win a luxurious Rolex Submariner Date Watch through another exciting raffle, with 1,100+ participants, adding a touch of another level of luxury to the event. As attendees felt the dynamic atmosphere of ICE London 2024, anticipation soared for the chance to own this iconic timepiece. Crafted with precision and renowned for its elegance, the Rolex Submariner Date Watch epitomized the essence of sophistication and prestige.
