Sports betting risk & fraud management

Risk management in sports betting is a critical aspect of ensuring the stability and profitability of operations. It involves the development and implementation of processes, strategies, and contingency plans to identify, assess, and mitigate risks effectively.

The trading team covers all types of sports in pre-match and over 90,000 Live Events per month. Our Pre-Match Department offers odds on over 100+ sports and events, 15,000 leagues worldwide and over 3,000 betting markets and uses the latest fraud prevention and data analysis tools to safeguard against any market manipulation.

We take pride in our state-of-the-art support and products, including:
  • Identification of dangerous multi-accounts
  • Detection of players with risky behavior
  • Bonuses to some players during the game
  • Real-time response for the coincidence of set algorithms and parameters
  • Information security management
